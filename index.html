Biography

I am a master's student under the supervision of Prof. Mingfeng He and A.P. Qiuhui Pan at the School of Mathematical Science, Dalian University of Technology. I major in Applied Mathematics and belong to Complex System Modeling Laboratory.

Previously, I received my bachelor's degree in Economics Statistics with the outstanding graduate honor from a special program of the School of Statistics and Mathematics, Shandong University of Finance and Economics, where I systematically studied statistics and mathematics, under the supervision of Dr. Zhenzhen Zhu.
