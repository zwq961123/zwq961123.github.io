Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Beihang University, part of the Key laboratory of Mathematics, Informatics and Behavioral Semantics. My mentors include Professor Shaoting Tang and Assistant Professors Xin Wang and Longzhao Liu.

Previously, I completed my Master's degree in Applied Mathematics at Dalian University of Technology, under the guidance of Professor Mingfeng He and Associate Professor Qiuhui Pan. I earned a Bachelor's degree in Economic Statistics from Shandong University of Finance and Economics. Thus, I possess a diverse academic background in Mathematics, Statistics, and Economics.
